# KinCircle User Guide
**For Family Caregivers**

Welcome to KinCircle! This guide will help you use the app to track your caregiving contributions and stay organized.

---

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Open the app** in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. You'll see a welcome wizard asking for:
   - **Patient Name**: The person you're caring for
   - **Hourly Rate**: How much one hour of caregiving is worth (e.g., $20/hour)
   - **Security PIN**: Required to unlock the app and encrypt local data
3. Click **"Finish Setup"** to enter the app

---

## Key Features

### üìù Adding Expenses or Time

**To track money you spent:**
1. Click **"Add Entry"** in the sidebar
2. Select **"Expense"**
3. Enter the amount (e.g., $45.67)
4. Describe what it was for (e.g., "Groceries for Mom")
5. Click **"Add Entry"**

**To track time you spent:**
1. Click **"Add Entry"**
2. Select **"Time"**
3. Enter how many hours (e.g., 2.5 hours)
4. Describe what you did (e.g., "Doctor appointment with Dad")
5. Click **"Add Entry"**

üí° **Tip**: You can also take a photo of receipts or use voice recording!

**Receipt scanning (camera):**
1. In **Add Entry**, tap **"Receipt / Invoice"**
2. Allow camera access when prompted
3. Snap a photo ‚Äî the app will auto-fill details

## üîê Privacy & Security

### Setting Your PIN
1. Open **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Scroll to **"Custom PIN Code"**
3. Enter a 4-digit PIN twice
4. Click **"Update PIN"**
5. **Remember this PIN!** You'll need it to unlock the app

### Privacy Mode
If you're worried about sensitive information:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Check **"Enhanced Privacy Masking"**
3. This removes names, emails, and phone numbers before using AI features

---

## üé® Appearance

Choose how KinCircle looks on this device:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Under **Appearance**, pick **System**, **Light**, or **Dark**
3. Your preference is saved automatically

## üìÖ Managing Care Tasks

### Adding Tasks
1. Click **"Care Tasks"** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button to add a task
3. Fill in:
   - Task name (e.g., "Pick up prescription")
   - Who should do it
   - When it's due
4. Click the **+** button to save

### Converting Tasks to Time Entries
When you complete a task:
1. Mark it complete (click the circle)
2. Click **"Log to Ledger"**
3. Choose **Care Hours** or **Expense**, then **Save Entry**

## üìÅ Document Vault

### Storing Important Documents
1. Click **"Document Vault"**
2. Click **"+ Add Document"**
3. Enter:
   - Document name
   - Type (Medical, Legal, Financial, etc.)
   - Upload or link to the file
4. Click **"Add Document"**

### Emergency Mode
In an emergency (911 situation):
1. Click **"Emergency Mode"**
2. Enter your PIN to unlock Emergency Responder Mode
3. The screen turns high-contrast for first responders
4. They can quickly find medical history, medications, and contacts

---

## üíæ Backing Up Your Data

### Exporting Your Records
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to **"Manual Data Sync"**
3. Click **"Export Backup"**
4. Save the file to your computer

**When to backup:**
- Once a week
- Before any major changes
- Before sharing data with family

### Sharing with Other Family Members
1. Export your data (see above)
2. Email the backup file to siblings
3. They can import it by clicking **"Import Backup"** in their Settings

---

## üÜò Common Issues

### "The app looks empty after I opened it"
- You may need to run **"Cloud Sync"** in Settings to upload your data (one-way migration)
- For ongoing sharing, export a backup and import it on other devices

### "I forgot my PIN"
- If you have access to the app on another device, check Settings
- Otherwise, you may need to reset the app data (ask for technical help)

### "Voice recording isn't working"
- Make sure your browser has microphone permission
- Try using Chrome or Edge instead of Safari
